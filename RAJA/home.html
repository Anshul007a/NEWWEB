Message of Chairman

As a chairman of Shri Guru Nanak Education Society, I feel very delighted to see the small seedling sown in the year - 1990 with the promise to light the lamp of knowledge through education now it has become a fruitful tree. We at Shri Gurunanak Sr. Sec. School strive to offer much more then more education. We lay immense emphasis on the overall development of every child through physical. Mental and spiritual training in an attempt, to make these young minds grow up,not just into fine educated gentlemen but men who have the powers to make a difference, so that today's prodigies grow up to become tomorrow's leaders.
